About

The Riverside Opera Company is a Staten Island based not for profit arts organization founded in 1996 and has been consistently producing fully or semi-staged costumed and/or concert versions of musically diverse public programs with a professional orchestra ever since.

The ROC also provides FREE educational outreach and concert tickets to all students and children. Its current Artistic Director, Maestro Alan Aurelia,has conducted in Italy as well as in Carnegie Hall and seeks new talent by holding annual auditions. Some of its audition winners have gone on to sing at the Metropolitan Opera and make careers for themselves in Europe.
